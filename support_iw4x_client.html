If You Do Not Own The Game On Steam

Getting Started:

  1. Download and Install qBittorrent.
  2. Open the full game torrent in qBittorrent, making sure to match these settings.
  3. Once your torrent says Seeding, click on the torrent and hit the pause button.
  4. Download the IW4x archive
  5. Once downloaded, click on the iw4x archive in your downloads to open it
  6. On the top toolbar, click "Extract All"
  7. In the extraction wizard, fill in the location in which you downloaded the game.
    If you followed the tutorial closely up to this point, it should be C:/Games/IW4x
  8. Click Extract and wait for the extraction to finish
  9. You're all done! To start the game, double-click IW4x.exe.
    Your folder should look something like this. If it does not, you missed a step in the tutorial. Please try again carefully before requesting assistance.

To change your name, open the in-game console (press the ~ key) and type name yourname.

To unlock all, open the in-game console (press the ~ key) and type unlockstats.

If You Do Own The Game On Steam, And want DLC/IW4x.

Getting Started:

IMPORTANT: This only works with English copies of the game, so please make sure you have your game in english before preceding.

  1. Download and Install qBittorrent.
  2. Open the DLC torrent in qBittorrent, making sure to match these settings.
  3. Once your torrent says Seeding, click on the torrent and hit the pause button.
  4. Download IW4x and unzip it's contents into your game directory.
  5. To start the game, double-click IW4x.exe.

To change your name, open the in-game console (press the ~ key) and type name yourname.

To unlock all, open the in-game console (press the ~ key) and type unlockstats.
